Bio

An English rock band formed in London in 1965, Pink Floyd started as Syd Barrett, Nick Mason, Roger Water, and Richard Wright. Piper at the Gates of Dawn was their first album released in 1967.

Members:

  • Roger Waters - Bass Guitar
  • David Gilmour - Guitar
  • Nick Mason - Drum Kit
  • Richard Wright - Keyboard
  • Syd Barrett - Guitar 1965-1968
